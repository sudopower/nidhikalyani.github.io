About Me

Nidhi Kalyani is an aspiring CIPP/E & AIGP certified professional and a dedicated Data Privacy & Tech Law Enthusiast. With over two years of experience as a Legal Associate at INDIALAW LLP, Nidhi has a strong background in both litigation and non-litigation matters, including drafting and reviewing Privacy Policies, Data Processing Agreements (DPAs), Software License Agreements, and SaaS Agreements.

Successfully completed CIPP/E training and Hands-on training on DPO from World Cyber Security Forum (WCSF) and certified in PrivacyOps by Securiti.ai, equipping her with hands-on knowledge of GDPR, data subject rights, DPO roles, DPIAs, and Privacy by Design. Nidhi is passionate about combining her legal background with emerging global data privacy standards and looks forward to contributing effectively in international roles, with a focus on AI governance and cyber law.
